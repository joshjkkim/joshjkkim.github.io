Description

When I was applying to colleges it was actually horrible of a process in terms of organizing
keeping track of dates and what not.
So I ended up making a webscraping bot that would fetch all sorts of demographics, deadlines, admissions information and requirements
for thousands of colleges. Then I was able to make accounts with firebase that was able to add these colleges to your profile and make a custom curated
checklist for that specific college with the ability to customize and add/delete entries of course
That kind of spiraled into a bunch of other features like college comparison, major stats, essay topics/ideas, extracurricular inspirations, calendars and deadlines etc
MongoDB for their NoSQL style database which was very helpful for me and the types of data I was storing
